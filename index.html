Short bio

Philippe Tanguy is associate professor at Université de Bretagne Sud (UBS). He teach at the Université de Bretagne Sud in the UFR SSI. He performs his research activities at Lab-STICC in the MOCS team. He had a PhD in Electronics and digital communication at IETR. Currently, his research activities are dedicated to IoT system with a focus on the Cyber Security issues.
